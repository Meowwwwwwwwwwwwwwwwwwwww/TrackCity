TᖇᗩᑕKᑕITY

TrackCity is an innovative online platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and government institutions. It empowers individuals to directly report issues, submit grievances, and offer suggestions to local authorities, fostering better communication and transparency within urban governance. Whether it’s a road repair request, waste management concerns, or a suggestion for community development, TrackCity provides a user-friendly interface that allows citizens to make their voices heard.
